About

This site is your go-to destination for exploring the Holland Code Test—also known as the RIASEC model—a proven tool for connecting personality types to career satisfaction. Our mission is simple: to make this powerful framework easy to understand and useful for anyone navigating career, education, or personal development.

Whether you're a student exploring your future, a job seeker considering your next move, or a counselor guiding others, our resources are designed to help you make sense of your RIASEC type and what it means for your path forward.

Our Mission

We believe that career success starts with self-knowledge. The Holland Code framework—centered around six personality types: Realistic, Investigative, Artistic, Social, Enterprising, and Conventional—can unlock clarity and confidence in decision-making. We aim to make this classic assessment accessible, practical, and relevant for modern life.

What You'll Find Here

  • Clear explanations of each of the six RIASEC types
  • Guides to interpreting your test results
  • Career recommendations based on your top personality codes
  • Articles on the history and psychology behind the Holland Code
  • Free tools and resources for further exploration
